Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with BRUXINNOV and our enterprise data solutions.

What is BRUXINNOV's typical engagement model?

We typically start with a 1-2 week Discovery phase to understand your business objectives and data landscape, followed by a Blueprint phase to design the target architecture. Then we move to a 4-6 week Pilot to deliver immediate value with 1-2 high-impact use cases, before scaling to full enterprise deployment with ongoing support and enablement.

Do you work remotely across the EU, US and MENA regions?

Yes, BRUXINNOV operates globally with a remote-first approach. We serve clients across Europe, North America, and MENA regions. For critical phases like Discovery workshops and go-live support, we can arrange on-site visits when needed. Our team is distributed across multiple time zones to provide optimal coverage for international clients.

Which Microsoft technologies does BRUXINNOV specialize in?

We are Microsoft Fabric specialists with deep expertise in Power BI, Azure Data Factory, Azure SQL, Databricks, Azure OpenAI, and Microsoft Purview. Our team holds advanced certifications in Azure Data & AI solutions and follows Microsoft's latest best practices for modern data platforms, including OneLake, Direct Lake, and Copilot integration.

What industries does BRUXINNOV serve?

BRUXINNOV serves enterprise clients across Manufacturing, FMCG, Telecommunications, Financial Services, Healthcare, and B2B SaaS. We have particular expertise in supply chain analytics, customer intelligence, financial reporting, and operational dashboards. Our solutions are designed to meet strict regulatory requirements including GDPR, SOX, and industry-specific compliance standards.

How does BRUXINNOV ensure data governance and security?

Security and governance are built into every solution we deliver. We implement role-based access control (RBAC), row-level security (RLS), data lineage tracking, and automated data quality monitoring. All solutions follow Microsoft's Zero Trust architecture and include data contracts, privacy-by-design principles, and comprehensive audit trails for regulatory compliance.

What kind of ROI can we expect from a BRUXINNOV engagement?

Our clients typically see 30-40% reduction in time-to-insight, 12-18% improvement in forecast accuracy, and 20-30% faster decision cycles. We measure ROI through specific KPIs agreed during the Discovery phase, including cost savings from automated reporting, revenue impact from better forecasting, and efficiency gains from self-service analytics adoption.

Does BRUXINNOV provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer flexible support packages including managed services, on-call support, and Center of Excellence establishment. We also provide training programs to upskill your internal teams, knowledge transfer sessions, and can embed with your team during the transition period to ensure sustainable adoption and continuous improvement.
